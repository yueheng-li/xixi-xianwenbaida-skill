# 角色视觉锚点 · 统一规范

> ⚠️ **铁律**：生成任何一集配图prompt时，角色描述必须严格使用以下锚点，不得随章节变化。每个角色只能有一个固定形象。

---

## 核心角色

### 闹闹

```
7-year-old chinese girl,
round face, missing one front tooth (visible when smiling),
oversized white lab coat (sleeves rolled up, hem reaching ankles),
transparent goggles on forehead,
always standing on a small stool to reach the lab table,
expression: calm, deadpan, slight smile at most,
hair: short black hair with a small red hair clip,
height: very short compared to all gods around her
```

### 哪吒

```
12-year-old boy appearance,
hair buns (two buns on top of head),
red ribbon (混天绫) floating behind him,
fire wheels (风火轮) under his feet (or placed under lab table),
round face, confident expression,
color theme: red and gold,
expression when confident: proud, chin up,
expression after messing up: head down, mumbling, ears red
```

### 孙悟空

```
monkey king, furry face, golden eyes,
golden armor,
golden staff (金箍棒) leaning against wall (with label "实验器材·请勿用于搅拌"),
headband (紧箍咒) on forehead,
expression: excited or shocked (no middle state),
color theme: gold and brown,
fur sticks up when shocked
```

### 嫦娥

```
classical chinese beauty, long flowing hair,
moon-white robes,
always accompanied by a jade rabbit (玉兔) wearing a mini lab coat,
often holding a frost-covered test tube (emitting cold mist),
expression: gentle smile (ALWAYS smiling, even when making sarcastic remarks),
color theme: moon-white and silver
```

### 八戒

```
pig demon, round belly,
lazy but occasionally lucky expression,
color theme: pink and brown,
often seen eating or reaching for food
```

### 二郎神

```
handsome god with three eyes,
third eye on forehead (opens to scan/analyze),
calm, data-driven expression,
color theme: silver and blue,
often holding a data tablet or surrounded by HUD displays
```

### 太白金星

```
elderly man with long white beard,
gentle, scholarly appearance,
 Taoist robes,
expression: wise but slightly overwhelmed by the chaos around him,
color theme: white and light gray
```

### 玉帝

```
majestic emperor,
dragon robes,
dignified expression that occasionally cracks,
color theme: gold and imperial yellow
```

## 场景统一规范

```
heavenly laboratory,
clouds visible through windows,
colorful test tubes on cloud-shaped lab benches,
whiteboard on wall,
safety poster on wall: "实验室安全守则·第X版" (X increases each episode due to accidents),
warm golden heavenly light,
bright and airy atmosphere,
ceilings very high (gods are much taller than 闹闹)

grand heavenly palace turned classroom,
golden pillars,
blackboard on one wall,
god-sized desks arranged in rows,
cloud ceiling,
warm imperial gold and red tones

moon palace cryogenic lab,
frost on everything,
ice crystal formations,
silver-white and pale blue tones,
jade rabbit assistant present,
cold mist floating at floor level
```

water curtain cave and peach orchard,

waterfall in background,
small monkeys watching from trees,

## 画风统一参数

所有集数配图prompt必须包含以下固定参数：

```
children's book illustration style,
bright warm colors,
Q版 cartoon style (head-to-body ratio approximately 1:2 to 1:3),
round soft shapes, no sharp edges,
expressive exaggerated faces,
ian-xiaohei illustration style,
vertical composition 9:16, 1080x1920, portrait,
kid-friendly, no realistic violence
```

---

## 使用规则

1. 每集生成配图prompt时，角色描述必须从本文档**逐字复制**对应角色的英文锚点
2. 场景描述必须从本文档的场景规范中选取
3. 画风参数必须**完整附加**到每个prompt末尾
4. 新角色首次出场时，必须先在本文件中补充其视觉锚点，再用于prompt
5. 禁止在单集prompt中临时修改角色外观（如"这次悟空穿了一双新鞋"）——所有修改必须先在本文档更新
