### 场景 01 · 天庭实验室（主场景）

**中文说明**：闹闹上课的主教室。实验室全景，镜头在门口位置正对讲台方向。云窗可见蓝天白云，云形实验台上摆满彩色试管。白板墙上有"实验室安全守则·第X版"海报。暖金色天光洒满整个空间。天花板极高（神仙比闹闹高很多）。无人物在场——这是一张空镜全景。

**Prompt**：

```
empty bright science lab, no characters present, wide establishing shot, camera at doorway height looking toward podium.

floor-to-ceiling cloud windows on the back wall, blue sky and white clouds visible outside, warm golden heavenly light streaming in. cloud-shaped lab benches in the foreground, colorful test tubes and beakers arranged on them.

whiteboard on the right wall with some chinese science notes and diagrams. safety poster on the left wall with chinese text "实验室安全守则·第3版" and cartoon illustrations.

a small wooden stool placed in front of the podium (this is where 闹闹 stands). the podium is a low cloud-shaped desk with green leaf notes scattered on it.

very high ceilings with golden light fixtures, bright and airy atmosphere, warm and inviting.

children's book illustration style, bright warm colors, Q版 cartoon style, round soft shapes, clean lines, ian-xiaohei illustration style, vertical composition 9:16, 1080x1920, portrait, kid-friendly.
```

## 使用流程

### 新章节开始时

```
1. 从本文件复制对应角色的"角色锚点速查表"描述
2. 粘贴到章节的配图/动画prompt中（一字不改）
3. 生成图片时，以单人定妆照作为 ian-xiaohei 的参考图
4. 生成动画时，以单人定妆照作为豆包的图生视频起始帧
```

### 跨章节一致性检查

```
- [ ] 角色锚点描述是否从本文件逐字复制？
- [ ] 画风参数是否与"画风统一参数"一致？
- [ ] 定妆照参考图是否已喂给模型？
- [ ] 与其他章节同角色的形象是否一致？
```
